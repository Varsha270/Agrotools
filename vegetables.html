The Benefits of Eating Fresh Vegetables

Eating fresh vegetables is essential for a balanced diet. They provide vitamins, minerals, and fiber while being low in calories. Incorporating a variety of vegetables into your meals can enhance your health and boost your immune system.

How to Store Fresh Vegetables

Proper storage of fresh vegetables is crucial for maintaining their freshness. Keep leafy greens in a damp paper towel and store them in a container in the fridge. Root vegetables like carrots and potatoes should be kept in a cool, dark place.

Top 5 Fresh Vegetables to Include in Your Diet

  • Spinach: Packed with iron and vitamins.
  • Bell Peppers: High in vitamin C.
  • Broccoli: Rich in fiber and antioxidants.
  • Carrots: Great for your eyes!
  • Tomatoes: Full of lycopene, a powerful antioxidant.
